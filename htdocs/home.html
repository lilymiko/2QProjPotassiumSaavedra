About Impact ✦ Archive

Impact Archive, stylized as "Impact ✦ Archive", is a collection of information for, from, and about the game Genshin Impact. Genshin Impact is a free-to-play open-world RPG game developed and published by miHoYo in September 2020. It classifies as an action and a fantasy game. Genshin Impact is available on Android, iOS, Microsoft Windows, Playstation 4, and Playstation 5.

As of February 7, 2022, Impact ✦ Archive is complete!
